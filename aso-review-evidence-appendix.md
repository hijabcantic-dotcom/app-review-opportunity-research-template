Do not present a review cluster as proof that a feature will win. Keep the source evidence, compare close alternatives, and use the result to guide interviews or experiments.

## Questions for customer interviews

Use these after the review pass. Ask about an actual recent situation, not whether someone likes a proposed feature.

1. "Tell me about the last time you tried to [job]. What happened?"
2. "What did you do when [failure] happened?"
3. "How often does that happen, and what does it cost you?"
4. "What have you tried instead?"
5. "What would have to change for you to switch or pay for a better option?"
